LOGO USAGE
----------

• Use logo-white on light backgrounds
• Use logo-transparent when compositing over imagery
• Minimum size: 32px (favicon only), 128px all other uses
• Clear space: minimum 0.5× logo height on all sides
• Do NOT recolor, rotate, stretch or add drop shadows
